Frequently Asked Questions

How are donations used?

Your donations directly support our water projects, education initiatives, and community development programs. We ensure maximum impact with minimal administrative costs.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Smart NGO are tax-deductible. We provide receipts for all contributions.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes, you can set up monthly, quarterly, or annual donations through our payment system.

How do I track my donation's impact?

We provide regular updates through email and our website about project progress and impact stories.
